FAQ

Football lineup builder FAQ

What is a football lineup builder?

A football lineup builder is an online tool for placing players on a pitch, choosing a formation, and creating a visual team sheet or starting XI graphic. Xilytics does this directly in your browser, with real club kit colors, flags and substitutes.

Can I create a soccer formation for free?

Yes. Xilytics is a free soccer formation creator. You can build and customize a full team lineup with no sign-up and no payment.

Which football formations can I use?

Xilytics supports popular formations including 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 4-2-3-1, 4-1-4-1, 3-5-2, 3-4-3, 5-3-2 and many more common football tactics.

Can I export my lineup as an image?

Yes. After building your football lineup you can save the team graphic as a high-resolution PNG image with your team crest, ready to share on social media.

Can I make a Starting XI graphic?

Yes. Xilytics is designed for starting XI graphics, matchday lineups, fantasy squads and tactical previews.

Can I customize player names, numbers, kits and flags?

Yes. Click any player to edit their name and number, add their country flag, link multiple substitutes, and choose real club kit colors and patterns.

Does it work in my language?

Yes. Xilytics is available in 7 languages: Türkçe, English, Español, Deutsch, Français, Português and Italiano.
